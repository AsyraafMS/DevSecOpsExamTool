P

Practice Mode

Instant feedback after every question with full explanations. Ideal while you learn.

  • Immediate answer reveal
  • Pick number of questions
  • Optional category filter
E

Exam Mode

Timed, exam-like conditions. Answers and explanations are revealed only at the end.

  • Configurable timer
  • Mark for review & navigate
  • Score & pass/fail summary
R

Revision Mode

Drill the questions you've previously gotten wrong or flagged for review.

  • Auto-collected from past attempts
  • Skip what you've mastered
  • Reset the queue any time
M

Mock Exam Mode

11 official-style mock exams, 40 questions each, drawn from the published DSOF question pool.

  • Pick exam 1 through 11
  • Timed, exam-like delivery
  • Per-mock scoring & review
F

Flash Card Mode

Untimed spaced study. See each question, reveal the answer, and rate how well you knew it.

  • All 440 mock questions or one deck
  • Self-rate: Again / Hard / Good
  • Replay the cards you missed
